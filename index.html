RULES

This game is played with two players on a single tab, but you can just play against yourself. Here are the rules.




1. A dice is rolled and that number is added to your turn's score, you can decide to roll again or end your turn.


2. If you end your turn, the points you mustered this turn is added to your total score, and it becomes the other player's turn.


3. You can keep rolling the dice instead of ending your turn, this will allow you to add more points to your turn's score, but if you roll a one, you will lose all your turn's points and your turn will end.


4. The first player to 100 points wins!
